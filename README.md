# 🛒 Woolworths Customer Purchase & Market Basket Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Woolworths retail transaction data to identify customer purchasing behaviour, product performance, and cross-selling opportunities using **Microsoft Excel**.

The analysis combines **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Correlation Analysis, and Dashboard Visualization** to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights and actionable recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Analyze product sales performance
- Identify the highest revenue-generating products
- Evaluate customer purchasing patterns
- Discover complementary product relationships
- Provide recommendations for product bundling and cross-selling strategies

---

## 📊 Revenue Analysis

- **Diapers** generated the highest revenue (**$23,625**), making them the most valuable product category.
- **Wet Wipes** contributed the second-highest revenue (**$19,218**).
- **Bread** and **Milk** consistently performed well, indicating strong everyday customer demand.

---

## 📦 Quantity Analysis

- **Wet Wipes** recorded the highest purchase volume (**4,045 units**).
- **Bread** and **Milk** ranked among the most frequently purchased products.
- **Salsa** had the lowest sales volume and revenue.

---

## 🔍 Market Basket Analysis

Correlation analysis identified several complementary purchasing patterns that can support cross-selling strategies.

| Product Combination | Business Opportunity |
|----------------------|--------------------------------|
| Pasta + Pasta Sauce | Bundle promotions |
| Nachos + Salsa | Shelf placement optimization |
| Diapers + Wet Wipes | Family essentials campaign |
| Milk + Bread | Everyday combo offers |

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Wet Wipes recorded the highest purchase volume, indicating strong recurring customer demand.
- Diapers generated the highest overall revenue despite lower sales volume.
- Pasta and Pasta Sauce showed a strong purchasing relationship, making them suitable for bundled promotions.
- Nachos and Salsa demonstrated complementary buying behaviour.
- Correlation analysis provides valuable insights for merchandising and promotional planning.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Bundle **Pasta and Pasta Sauce** to increase average basket value.
- Position **Nachos and Salsa** together to encourage complementary purchases.
- Promote **Diapers and Wet Wipes** through family-focused marketing campaigns.
- Introduce **Milk and Bread combo offers** to drive repeat purchases.
- Improve **Salsa visibility** through discounts and bundle promotions.
